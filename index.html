The HELPING ARM ARMY- All in One for the help of less fortunate sections of society.

The HELPING ARM ARMY is a Volunteer-based Zero funds organization that works to get surplus food from restaurants & parties to less fortunate sections of society in cities across India and 14 other countries. The organization consists of over 78,262 volunteers (approximately) in 204 cities, and have served food to over 66.17 million people so far. The volunteers people comes from all walks of life. The less fortunate sections who the organization reaches out to include homeless families, orphanages, night shelters, homes for abandoned children, patients from public hospitals, etc

  • Beat global hunger and bring out the best of humanity using food as a medium. The idea is to create self-sustained chapters across the world who will look after their local community. And in the process, inspire people around us to give back to those who need it most.
  • Vaccination drive are been organized by our volunteers for underprivileged members of our society who don't have much idea about it ,in various part of the country to protect their life from Covid-19.
  • The HELPING ARM ACADEMY is their to be a bridge between the street and schools, by giving our children the tools and knowledge to go to school.The Helping Hand Academy empowers 7328+ street children with basic primary education. Our Volunteers conduct regular weekend classes and excursions with a standardized curriculum to introduce and drive the spirit of learning.s
